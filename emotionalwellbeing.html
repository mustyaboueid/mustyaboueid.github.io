WHat is emotional wellbeing 

Emotional Wellbeing also relates to mental health. As it can affect your mental health. It's the ability to handle both negative and positive emotions. Also how aware you are of these emotions that you may feel. A person's emotional wellbeing can affect their overarching decision making and relationships. Being emotionally healthy is being able to cope with stressful and difficult times. A sign of being emotionally healthy is knowing when to seek professional help.

Signs of being emotionaly unhealthy

  • Singling yourself out. Distancing yourself from the people around you.
  • Not getting enough sleep/sleeping too much.
  • Feeling guilty, worthless or hopeless.
  • Not taking care of your hygiene.

Waysto improve emotional wellbeing

  • Do not be a stranger to friends and family. Plan time to spend with them whether it is online or in person.
  • There are many ways to improve sleeping habits. Relax before bed and when going to sleep block out light and the noise surrounding you.
  • Find something you are passionate about. Like a job or caring for a pet. Find something that makes you happy to do.
  • Limit your bed for sleep only. You want your mind to associate your bed with sleep.
  • Set goals for the day that are easily achievable. Like Make your bed,eat breakfast, brush teeth, exercise for 20 minutes, get some work done.
