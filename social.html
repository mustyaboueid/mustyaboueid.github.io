Social health is our interactions and relationships with others. It also umbrellas aspects of social interactions such as.

A common cause of mental illnesses are drug and alcohol abuse, traumatic or a stressful event. They can affect mental well being. Genetics can also have a factor in the matter as well.

social relationships

The relationship with family, friends and communities. Ways to improve one's social relationships are making yourself available to spend time with your social network. Staying connected with people even if it is a simple message or call once a day.

Social Skills

How well someone can communicate with people and have positive interactions. This will also affect how people will see you as a person and a colleague. Enhancing social skills can be as easy as being polite and giving compliments and showing active listening through your body language by facing the person or people you are talking to.

Social Participation

Social participation is the act of people in a community coming together and volunteering, having small events or joining clubs within the community. Joining a social group with similar interests as you in your local community is a way to improve your social participation.
