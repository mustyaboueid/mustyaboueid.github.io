WHat is meantsl health

Mental health is a state of mind where people are able to cope with the struggles of life. Mental health affects one's individual ability to make reasonable decisions and build relationships. Mental health can change all the time.

Cause Of mental health

A common cause of mental illnesses are drug and alcohol abuse, traumatic or a stressful event. They can affect mental well being. Genetics can also have a factor in the matter as well.
